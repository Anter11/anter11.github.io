About Me

I got my start in early 2016 when somebody on Discord posted Zēm Musu Kājām on FBIV's channel. I dug deeper and found vilki.lv, a whole site full of Latvian Legion music. I finally got around to making videos on June 2020 with a translated Kad ar Uzvaru, a legion song.

At some point I was recomended Jadna Bosno Suverena on Youtube. I made a playlist of Yugoslav war songs, but when I checkd back on it one day I saw that most of the videos got deleted. From that point on I made it my goal to archive all of the songs, so that they never get lost again.

Much of the Serbian work in particular orginially came from the Vučjak site. Vučjak is long inactive, but his videos show up on Youtube once in a while. I intend to carry on his legacy. Everything in my archive (songs and videos) are publically availiable on this site, so that everybody has access to them.
